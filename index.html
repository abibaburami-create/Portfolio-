🏆 Caterpillar Tech Challenge 2026 Hardware · IoT
OILIQ — Engine Oil Intelligence & Condition Monitoring
Real-time engine oil health monitoring system for Caterpillar machinery. Monitors TBN, oxidation, soot, fuel dilution, and temperature using inline sensors. Computes an Oil Health Index (OHI) and Remaining Useful Life (RUL) on an STM32 microcontroller — no cloud dependency. Sends SMS alerts via ESP32 at critical thresholds. Total BOM: ₹5,020.
STM32 ESP32 PCB Design Sensor Integration Circuit Design MQTT
Circuit · Sensor · PCB
💡 TNWISE 2026 — Women's Hackathon IoT · Digital Twin
Digital Twin for Industrial Motor System
Built a real-time digital twin prototype for industrial motor systems under the theme "Inclusive Digital Transformation." Uses an ESP32 for data acquisition, a relay module for fault simulation, and Blynk IoT for cloud dashboard visualization. Implements rule-based predictive maintenance to detect abnormal motor states before failure — a scalable, low-cost solution (₹800–1200) for manufacturing and water supply industries.
ESP32 Blynk IoT Digital Twin SolidWorks CAD Arduino IDE Predictive Maintenance
Team Member

Energy Conservation for Climate Resilience
Research project analyzing Delhi's air pollution crisis and proposing sustainable energy conservation strategies for Tamil Nadu. Studied air pollution causes, energy consumption patterns, and preventive measures — culminating in a data-driven roadmap for clean energy adoption.
